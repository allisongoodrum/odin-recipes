Butternut Squash Pasta

Description

This is a recipe for a no-fuss weeknight dinner. There is no babysitting your stove! Just chop and dump veggies into a pyrex, put into the oven, and boil some pasta and you're ready to eat. This feeds four people, or one service member.

Ingredients

  • butternut squash, one whole ~2 lbs., cubed
  • onion, 2 cut into quarter
  • garlic, 3 cloves smashed
  • olive oil, 1/2 cup
  • feta or goat cheese, 8oz
  • pasta, penne or similar, 1 lb.
  • honey, 1 tablespoon
  • sage, 2 teaspoons

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400Â°
  2. Add squash, onion, garlic, olive oil, and sage to a large ovensafe dish and toss to combine
  3. Season to taste with salt and pepper, and place goat cheese or feta in center, top with some olive oil
  4. Bake for about 40 minutes, stirring halfway through, and not disturbing the goat cheese
  5. Bring salted water to a boil, cook pasta as directed, and reserve 1/2 cup pasta water
  6. Stir reserved pasta water to dish with butternut squash
  7. Pour squash mixture into drained pasta, and serve
