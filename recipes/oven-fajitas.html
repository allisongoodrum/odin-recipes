Oven Fajitas

Description

This is a super easy recipe for delicious chicken fajitas. Dinner comes together in under an hour and feeds four people! Or just two people if you dine with my husband.

Ingredients

  • 3 bell peppers
  • 2 onions
  • 2 pounds chicken breast
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 lime
  • tortillas

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400Â°
  2. Cut bell peppers, onion, and chicken breast into strips
  3. Put peppers, onion, and chicken into a pyrex and add spices
  4. Toss all the ingredients together with the olive oil
  5. Place in preheated oven for 40 minutes, stirring half way through
  6. Squeeze lime over fajitas and serve with tortillas
